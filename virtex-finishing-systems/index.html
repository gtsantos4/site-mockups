About Virtex

35 Years of Delivering on the Most Demanding Schedules

Since 1990, Virtex Finishing Systems has been the painting and finishing contractor that general contractors in Central Virginia trust with their most important projects. We built our reputation on two things: exceptional quality and hitting every deadline.
