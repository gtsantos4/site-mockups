About the Museum

A Community Museum, Built for Everyone

The Virginia Discovery Museum has been part of Charlottesville since 1981. We moved to our permanent home on the Downtown Mall in 1990 and have been welcoming families here ever since.

Our mission is simple: foster intellectual curiosity and development for all children in our community. That word "all" is the one we take most seriously. Nearly a third of our visitors are admitted free through our Sponsored Admission program, supported by 63 social service agency partners.
