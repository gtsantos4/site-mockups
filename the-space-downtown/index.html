The Story

Two chefs. One space.
Thirty years of partnership.

Tim Burgess and Vincent Derquenne opened The Space in late 2010 at 210 West Water Street, the same address where they ran Metropolitain through 2003. The idea was simple: a venue built around the food, not the other way around.

Every event starts with a custom menu. Tim and Vincent work directly with each client to build something specific to the occasion, whether that's a seated five-course rehearsal dinner or passed hors d'oeuvres for a holiday gathering.

They also own Bizou and Bang, both within walking distance on the downtown mall.
