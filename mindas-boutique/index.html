About the Boutique

Meet Minda

Minda Gordon opened her boutique on Ivy Road in 1980 with a simple idea: women deserve clothing that's as unique as they are. No mass production. No fast fashion. Every piece hand-selected for its beauty, comfort, and craftsmanship.

Today, Minda still travels the world to find what you won't find anywhere else. From hand-blocked Anokhi prints to artisan-made butterfly wing earrings, every item in the shop has a story. Come in and she'll tell you about it.

Free consultations. No-pressure service. Walk in wearing your favorites and walk out with something that makes them even better.

Sourced Directly from Artisans

Minda travels to Thailand, India, and beyond to find pieces you won't see anywhere else in Charlottesville. She works directly with artisans, supporting traditional craftsmanship and bringing back clothing and accessories made with care.

Every sourcing trip means new arrivals in the shop. Follow along or sign up for email updates to know when new pieces land.

Giving Back

Minda Cares

Minda's Boutique is proud to support the Charlottesville community. Through the Minda Cares program, a portion of proceeds goes to local causes and charitable organizations. Shopping here means supporting both independent artisans abroad and your neighbors at home.
