What We Do

Full-Service Collision & Auto Care

From a fender bender to a full frame repair, we handle the job start to finish. OEM parts, DuPont paint, millimeter-level frame precision.

Collision Repair

Unibody work, frame straightening to millimeter precision, and pre- and post-repair alignment documentation so you see the difference.

Auto Refinishing

DuPont products and upgraded equipment for modern multi-layer finishes. Color matching that disappears the repair. Lifetime warranty.

Paintless Dent Repair

For dents that didn't crack the paint. Preserves the factory finish and saves you the cost and downtime of a full repaint.

24-Hour Towing

Accidents don't wait for business hours. Call us and we'll get your vehicle to the shop. After-hours towing through Charlottesville Wrecker Service.

Detailing & Waxing

Full interior and exterior detail. Hand wash, clay bar, polish, and wax. Bring it in looking like an accident and pick it up looking like the showroom.

Mechanical & A/C

Light mechanical work, air conditioning repair, tires and brakes. One shop handles everything so you're not running between three places.

About Masters

Built From a One-Car Garage on Main Street

Jeff Cotten started Masters Auto Body in 1982 in a small garage off Main Street in Charlottesville. Born and raised here. The current shop on Monticello Road opened in 1986, and two more locations followed: Little River, SC and Goochland, VA.

Most of the 14-person crew has been here over 12 years. The lead technicians have 25+ years in the trade. When someone stays that long, it says something about the shop.

Lifetime warranty on all paint and workmanship, for as long as you own the vehicle. We use OEM parts and DuPont refinishing products.

You Have the Right to Choose Your Own Shop

Your insurance company may suggest a shop. They cannot require it. Virginia law gives you the right to choose where your car gets repaired. Choose the shop that's been on Monticello Road since 1982.
