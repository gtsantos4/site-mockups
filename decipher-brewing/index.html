What We Brew

From Flagship to World-Class

From our signature Scottish Ale to World Beer Cup medal winners. Always something new on tap, always something worth coming back for.

Our flagship. Rich, malty, with caramel sweetness and a clean finish. The beer that started it all.

Our Story

Three Veterans. One Brewery.

Brad, Scott, and Megan Burton spent a combined 38 years in military service before opening Decipher Brewing in 2019. Brad served 9 years in the Navy, worked at a homebrew shop in Colorado, brewed professionally in California, and now teaches the Commercial Craft Brewing course at Piedmont Valley Community College. In the brewery.

Scott, also 9 years Navy, runs front of house, brews British and American styles, and mans the grill for his legendary Scott Dogs. Megan retired from the Air Force in 2022 after 20 years of service and handles operations. Together they've built a brewery that wins World Beer Cup medals and hosts the best Oktoberfest in Charlottesville.
