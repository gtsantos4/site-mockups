50 Years on Berkmar Drive

Creatures Great & Small has been grooming and boarding pets in Charlottesville since the 1970s. Owner Katie and her staff specialize in animals that other places turn away: the anxious ones, the reactive ones, the seniors who need a little extra patience.

Family-owned, women-owned, independent. The staff knows your pet by name and will drive to the vet to pick up their medication if they need it.

Tours are welcome by appointment. Come see the place before you leave your pet.
